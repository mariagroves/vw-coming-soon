Welcome to Vegan Wheels!

We are a Glasgow based cooperative where we aim to fund our passion for protecting animals through connecting all of Glasgow’s best vegan restaurants and cafes with a quick and efficient delivery service.

In the end, we hope to provide not only an amazing delivery service, but a platform for Glasgow’s vegan community to communicate and share ideas that will help the vegan movement grow and improve the quality of life for animals.

Web App

We hope to have the delivery side of things up and running with our web app by the end of October, with a few of Glasgow’s vegan places participating to start with and hopefully by the end of the year, every one of them will be on board. While we get set up, please keep an eye on this page to see where we are with our progress.

Get involved

If you would like to provide us with any pointers, we’d defo appreciate it! The more participation we get from you, fellow animal lovers, the more successful we’d be at harnessing that energy that our community has. Glasgow already has this strong and creative vegan community, it’s time to let its potential out and be as powerful and effective as it should be. And that’s what we’re all about.

Would you like to be a rider?

We are looking for physically fit, motivated and creative people to join our team of plant-powered delivery cyclists. Each position will be an active part in our cooperative so you’ll need brains as well as fitness. Innovative peeps with a strong desire for animal rights will be the ideal candidates. Salaries will be decided upon by everyone involved and according to profits.
